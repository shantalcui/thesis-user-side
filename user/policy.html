TERMS & POLICY


  • After reservation, you will be given 15 minutes to order. If failed to do so, your reservation will be void.
  • After paying for your meal, you will be given 5mins as an after-meal break. After the given time limit, the table will appear as vacant in our system.
  • To ensure that the customer will not attempt to leave the restaurant without paying, a PSN must be taken when creating an account.
  • If the customer attempts to leave without paying, a complaint will be filed at the Police Department with the customer's PSN and that customer will be banned from the restaurant.
